We accomodate a wide range of spices to meet your demands. EU certified organic variety is available for all products. Individually packaged samples are available on demand and enquiries from North America and EU are welcome as well.

We have a lab which can match your required product, as per your samples and composition data, using only naturally derived constituents. ​Lead-time is based on products: usually, can supply immediately. For the UK, LCL shipments via sea, it will take 30-32 days transit and FCL shipments take 25-27 days for transit.

We pack in PP bag, Paper bags, Carton boxes etc., based on customer requirement. Packing sizes: 50kg/30kg/25kg/20kg/10kg etc. We also do private labeling and small retail certified (including KLBD) packing. All packaging is done in Food grade materials.We provide a certificate of analysis for every lot we supply. During shipment for whole and ground spices, PQ certification is mandatory. For some products, Spice Board certificate is mandatory.
